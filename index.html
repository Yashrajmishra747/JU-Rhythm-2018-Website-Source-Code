What's JU-Rhythm anyway?

It is the best fest in Rajasthan that happens every year at JECRC University.

JU-Rhythm is going to be a Mega three day fun filled techno-cultural and business event. The objective of the fest is to promote youth culture, expand networking opportunities and to provide a platform for exchange of creative ideas.
